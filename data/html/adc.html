Dear ADC members,

As we near the end of 2004, the Apple Developer Connection would like to thank you for your support of Apple platforms and technologies this year. Your thoughtful feedback is greatly appreciated and has helped us continue to evolve ADC to support your success. This was a busy and productive year for Apple and for the ADC community. This special message recaps some of the highlights of 2004 and points to some of the latest resources available to our members.

Have a great holiday and we’ll see you in 2005!

Apple Developer Connection
ADC Reference Library:  With features like sortable and filterable lists, the ADC Reference Library makes it easier than ever for you to find and use Apple’s developer documentation resources.

Getting Started with Mac OS X: To help the many developers who are new to the Mac platform get up to speed fast on Apple technologies and topics, we added a new set of “Getting Started” documents.

RSS Feeds: We brought you the latest ADC Headlines as well as new and updated Reference Library Resources by launching two new RSS feeds. We also delivered RSS feeds for each of the developer mailings lists, making it even easier to stay active with the Mac development community.

Technical Documentation and Sample Code: To help accelerate your Mac OS X development, ADC published 102 new Technical Documents in 2004, along with 54 new pieces of Sample Code, and 130 new Technical Notes and Technical Q&As.

Feature Articles and Content: We’re committed to regularly publishing interesting new content about Apple technologies and the diverse Mac developer community. To that end, we published more than 30 new feature articles and developer profiles in 2004. Be sure to check out some of the most popular articles:
Optimizing Your Application with Shark 4
Introducing Xcode 1.5: Improving Speed and Workflow
Eclipse and Mac OS X: A Natural Combination
Xgrid: High Performance Computing for the Rest of Us
Simultaneous Development: Blizzard Entertainment Does Both At Once



Improved Software Download: We enhanced the Download Software area of the ADC Member Site this year, upgrading our globally distributed network to improve the performance of downloads for our members worldwide.

Latest Apple Hardware: In addition to adding a hardware discount benefit to the ADC Select membership, we offered ADC members several opportunities in 2004 to get promotional discounts on the latest Apple hardware for developing, testing, and demonstrating your Mac products.

Direct Access to Apple Engineers: This year, thousands of you received direct advice and assistance from Apple’s developer support engineers, helping you accelerate your development or incorporate the latest technologies in your products. ADC members worldwide also received exclusive invitations to dozens of hands-on workshops and technology briefings.

Apple Worldwide Developers Conference 2004: With 3500 attendees, almost 200 technical sessions, and more than 75 exhibitors, this was Apple’s biggest developer conference ever. The announcement and pre-release seeding of Tiger — the most important release for developers since the original Mac OS X debut in March 2001 — made WWDC 2004 one of the most exciting developer conferences ever. Stay tuned for information from the ADC about the upcoming WWDC 2005.

Early access to Tiger software and documentation: Our Select and Premier members were able to get started developing applications for Tiger this year with ongoing access to pre-release versions of Tiger and Xcode 2.0.

Tiger Session Highlights DVD: To help you incorporate innovative Tiger features into your projects, we offered Select and Premier members a free DVD featuring Mac OS X Tiger session videos from WWDC 2004 along with docs and sample code.

Tiger Tech Talks: Nearly 3000 ADC members worldwide have received detailed information on key Tiger technologies at more than a dozen Tiger Tech Talks. Our members Down Under should mark their calendars for the Sydney Tech Talk, taking place 3 and 4 February 2005.

Dashboard Widget Contest and exclusive video: Everything you need to create the next winning Widget — including access to the latest Dashboard Widget SDK and an exclusive Dashboard development video — is now available to ADC Select and Premier members.
